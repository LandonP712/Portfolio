About Me

Hello, my name is Landon Pauley I am a student athlete in the class of 2026, I attend Keswick Christian School and play on the varsity football team. I first played football at a little league organization known as the T Birds, I then took a break not knowing how much i loved the sport. By my 6th grade year i played on the middle school team and found a passion for the sport and continued to play through my last year in middle school. Along came my freshman year and that is when i found my love for the sport of football not only was it a game but it saved me and i felt like i was at home when i was on the field. My freshman summer i found how much of a passion i had and began to work and started setting goals and i accomplished those goals this year and im still shooting for a higher standard. Football taught me many lessons like how to push through pain and struggle to reach your goals and never be satisfied knowing you can do better.
